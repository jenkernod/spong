Limbo and Art History: The Role of German Expressionism in the Game's Design

This purpose of this project is to explore both the manifest and latent traces of German Expressionism in Limbo from the Danish Group Playdead. I assume no previous knowledge of either gaming or programming. The project will, however, explore some dimensions of music and sound in connection with my approach to the game. I recommend playing the game before reading and listening to this project as that will enhance your experience. Thank You!
